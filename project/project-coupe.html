Coupe Derivation | Form Developing & Clay Modeling

This is a form-developing project sponsored by SAIC Volkswagen, Shanghai China. We did research on external benchmarks, on the differences between Sedan and Coupe in exterior design and structures. Then we developed a Model-C Coupe based on the chassis of Phideon.

Design Project 2016 | 8 weeks
Tongji University, Shanghai China
Skill: vehicle exterior design, form exploration, calymodeling
Team: Wenting Xu
