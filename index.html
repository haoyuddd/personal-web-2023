Welcome to my website!

I am a designer/researcher, leverage my designerly skills and Design Thinking mindset in research, through hands-on prototyping, explorative design ideation and in-context evaluation. My interests include tangible and haptic interfaces, user experience, and interaction aesthetics.

Currently, I am pursuing my Ph.D. degree at TU Eindhoven in the Netherlands, focusing on in-vehicle interaction design in the field of automated driving. My research revolves around facilitating trust relationships between human users and advanced technology, as well as creating enjoyable user experiences. I take a research-through-design approach, led by the innovative and creative features of design activities, to conduct research.
