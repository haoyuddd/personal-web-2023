HAOYU
DONG

Design Researcher | Ph.D. Candidate


I leverage my designerly skills and Design Thinking mindset in my work through explorative design ideation and in-context evaluation. My interests include tangible and haptic interfaces, user experience, and human-technology interaction. I possess a strong aptitude for bringing together individuals with diverse backgrounds and fostering collaboration in an interdisciplinary manner.


I am currently pursuing my Ph.D. degree at TU Eindhoven in the Netherlands, focusing on in-vehicle interaction design in the field of smart mobility. My research revolves around facilitating trust relationships between human users and advanced technology, as well as creating enjoyable user experiences. I take a research-through-design approach, led by the innovative and creative features of design activities, to conduct research.
